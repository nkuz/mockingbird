About Us



Mockingbird Cafe owners Kathy and Tom West have been fixtures around the Somerset Hills for years. First known for their stylish catering at parties, they gained popularity on the a la carte dining scene while running the Limestone Cafe in Peapack. After the economy went south, the Wests went northeast, though only by a few miles. They downsized in August 2010 with the 11-table, 40-seat Mockingbird Cafe in the heart of Basking Ridge, on a corner that gets lots of foot traffic.
